BACK-2-SCHOOL is a fully integrated open platform for students from classes 3 to 12, to show their IQ and explore their skills. The competition uses a combination of children's natural desire to compete and participate effectively to hone their skills in competitions like MATH BEE and SPELL BEE. The event goes on for two days and aims at nurturing mathematical and vocabulary skills among children in a challenging environment. It encourages children to participate, on equal basis, solely with the aim of familiarizing them with the contest to achieve extraordinary levels of speed and accuracy with their skills. All the participants will be recognized with certificates and the top 3 students from each category will be rewarded with cash prizes. The best school gets honoration with a trophy.
